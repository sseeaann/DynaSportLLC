About Us


DynaSport was founded by enthusiasts to serve enthusiasts. There’s something special about BMW’s and MINI’s, and we believe there should be something special about the way your vehicle is serviced as well. As BMW and MINI owners ourselves, we understand what’s behind the wheel.

DynaSport offers full service, repairs, stock and performance parts, and performance modifications for all 2002 and newer MINI Coopers and all ranges of BMW’s. From simple oil changes to track preparations, every BMW and MINI receives our professional attention.

Performance Matters


Automotive manufacturers spend millions developing vehicle systems. Adding the incorrect performance component can actually detract from your car’s performance and reliability. At DynaSport, we do more than just bolt on go-fast parts. We design performance systems to best suit your driving needs. Working with top level performance manufacturers allows us to create synergistic systems to fulfill your performance goals. Feel free to call or come by to talk to us about unlocking your BMW & MINI’s full performance potential.
